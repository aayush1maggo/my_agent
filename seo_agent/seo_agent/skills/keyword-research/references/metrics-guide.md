# Keyword Metrics Interpretation Guide

## Search Volume
| Range | Signal |
|---|---|
| 10,000+/mo | High-volume, usually high competition — worth targeting only with strong authority |
| 1,000–9,999/mo | Mid-volume — good balance of traffic potential and achievability |
| 100–999/mo | Low-to-mid — often realistic targets for new or growing sites |
| 10–99/mo | Long-tail — low competition, high intent, quick wins |
| < 10/mo | Niche — only target if very high commercial intent or exact match to a service |

## Keyword Difficulty (KD) — SEMrush Scale 0–100
| KD Score | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 0–29 | Easy | Low competition — achievable with good on-page SEO and a few links |
| 30–49 | Possible | Moderate effort needed — solid content + some backlinks required |
| 50–69 | Hard | Established sites dominate — needs authority and strong content |
| 70–84 | Very Hard | Top sites with thousands of backlinks — significant link building needed |
| 85–100 | Extremely Hard | Major brands only — avoid unless domain authority is 60+ |

## Keyword Tiers for Prioritisation
**Tier 1 — Quick Wins (target now)**
- KD < 30 AND volume > 50/mo
- OR client already ranks positions 11–30 (one optimisation push needed)

**Tier 2 — Mid-Term (3–6 months)**
- KD 30–60
- Requires: quality content, 5–15 supporting backlinks, internal linking

**Tier 3 — Long-Term (6–12+ months)**
- KD > 60
- Requires: topical authority, aggressive link building, strong brand signals

## CPC (Cost Per Click)
- High CPC (> $5) = high commercial intent — prioritise for service/product pages
- Medium CPC ($1–5) = mixed intent — good for blog + service hybrid content
- Low CPC (< $1) = informational — good for top-of-funnel blog content

## Competition Score (SEMrush)
- This is paid search competition (0–1), not organic difficulty
- High competition + high CPC = keyword is commercially valuable even if organically hard
- Use it to identify keywords worth targeting on both SEO and PPC channels

## Trend Direction
- Rising trend: growing search demand — invest now before competition increases
- Stable: consistent volume — safe long-term target
- Declining: fading interest — avoid unless it's core to the business

## Authority Score (Domain Authority — SEMrush)
| Score | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 0–20 | New/low authority site |
| 21–40 | Developing authority |
| 41–60 | Established mid-authority |
| 61–80 | Strong authority |
| 81–100 | Major brand (Wikipedia, Forbes, etc.) |

A client should primarily target keywords where the top 3 organic results have AS within 20 points of their own domain.
