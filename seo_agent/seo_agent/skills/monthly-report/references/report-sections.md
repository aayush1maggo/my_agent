# Monthly SEO Report — Standard Sections

Use this section order for every Google Doc report. Use plain text only (no Markdown).

---

## Document Title
[Client Name] SEO Report — [Month YYYY]

---

## Section 1: Overview & Highlights
3–5 bullet points max. Written for a non-technical reader (client/stakeholder).
- Traffic direction: up/down/flat with % change
- Best result this month
- Key concern or risk identified
- Most important next step

---

## Section 2: Traffic Performance (GA4)
Paragraph summary + metrics table.

Metrics to include:
- Organic sessions (vs previous period)
- Total users
- Engagement rate
- Conversions / leads (if tracked)
- Top traffic channel breakdown

Note: if GA4 data is not available for this section, write "Data pending — will be updated" and leave placeholder rows.

---

## Section 3: Search Visibility (Search Console)
Paragraph summary + query/page tables.

Include:
- Total clicks (vs previous month)
- Total impressions
- Average CTR
- Average position
- Top 5 queries by clicks
- Top 5 pages by clicks
- Notable position movements (up or down more than 5 spots)

---

## Section 4: Work Completed This Month
Sourced from Basecamp todos — use generate_basecamp_structured_summary output.

Subheadings:
- On Page / Technical
- Off Page
- Local SEO
- Other

List each completed task as a plain sentence. Include brief impact note where known.

---

## Section 5: Keyword Rankings Update
List target keywords with current position vs last month.

| Keyword | Last Month | This Month | Change |
|---|---|---|---|

Highlight: improved, declined, new entries in top 10.

---

## Section 6: Observations & Insights
2–4 paragraphs of analysis connecting the data to business outcomes.
- What does the traffic pattern tell us about the audience?
- Are there seasonal patterns affecting these numbers?
- What is the biggest opportunity not yet captured?

---

## Section 7: Recommendations & Next Steps
Numbered list — maximum 5 items. Each item must include:
- The action
- Why it matters (data-backed)
- Who owns it
- Rough timeline

Example format:
1. Optimise title tags on the top 3 service pages (currently > 65 chars, being truncated in SERPs). Expected: +5–10% CTR improvement. Owner: SEO team. Target: next 2 weeks.

---

## Section 8: Next Month's Focus
2–3 sentences on the primary strategic focus for next month based on the findings above.
